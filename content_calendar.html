These are your 5 permanent series + 1 pilot. Each one becomes a recognizable container your audience returns to.

Season 23 Pilot
Personal Documentary · Seasonal
Your era-opening series. A birthday-keyed chapter system. The pilot season sets the tone for everything that follows — introspective, archival, honest. This is your flagship format.
No Signal Maps
Travel · Ongoing
Your travel series. Named for going somewhere without a plan, trusting the place to tell you where to look. Covers country guides, travel essentials, and solo adventure dispatches. Cinematic over listicle.
Show Up Reps
Sports + Active Life · Ongoing
Gym, swim, bouldering, golf, hiking with a sports lens. Not a fitness channel — a life channel where movement is part of the texture. Named for the unsexy consistency of just showing up.
Founder's Dispatch
Startup Life · Ongoing
The co-founder content thread. Building in public, the real version. Day-in-the-life meets fly-on-the-wall documentary. Shows what it actually looks like to be 23 and building something.
Base Camp
Hiking + Outdoor · Ongoing
Your hiking and camping series. Named for the place before the climb. Reflective essays set in nature — solo hikes, summits, beach resets. Slow, cinematic, earned.
The Monologue
Motivational Talking Head · Ongoing
One camera. One take. One idea. Your direct-to-lens format for the thoughts worth saying out loud. No B-roll. No music. Just your voice and a fully-formed thought. Drops whenever a real one arrives.
